About me

If you ever hear my friends call me a 'Joburg boet' despite what you would think because of my accent, it's actually the truth. I was born in Gauteng and lived there up until the age of 11, which is when we moved to Cape Town. You know I actually thought Gauteng is such a nice place to live... but then I got a taste of the mother city and I'm telling you, I'll never move back. My mother originally came from Cape Town so she wasn't happy about living in Gauteng. She always said that the only nice things about Gauteng was the weather and the road to Cape Town. I want to be a Computer programmer one day, not necessarily a website developer though which is ironic since that is exactly what I'm sitting here doing. I just want to write programs for a company one day. I also enjoy playing rugby but do not intend to play after school.


Experience/Education

When we were living in Gauteng I attended Hennopspark Primary School. I completed grade 4 there and then we moved to Cape Town. After thoroughly evaluating our options, my parents sent me to Eversdal Primary School. Before I could blink twice I had to choose which high school I was going to attend. I don't think I really had a choice because my brother was in Stellenberg, but I don't regret going there at all, after all, if I didn't go there, I wouldnt have the opportunity to create my own website. I intend to matriculate next year and then study at the university of Stellenbosch.
